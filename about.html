A-01 · Our story

Twenty years of doing it the right way.

DS Solid Construction is a family-run home builder that has spent two decades building across central Iowa. The reputation that came with those homes was earned one house at a time.

We build homes that exceed expectations, with quality craftsmanship and personal attention on every detail. From the first lot walk to the final handoff, we stay involved, because the people who buy our homes are often neighbors and referrals, and we plan to keep it that way.

That hands-on approach is also why we stand behind our work long after the keys change hands. When something needs attention, we show up.
